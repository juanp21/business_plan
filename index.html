Risk Analysis & Mitigation

Client Acquisition & Retention Risk

Challenge: Failure to convince enough brands to switch from familiar stock music platforms

Mitigation: PSG validation demonstrates brand preference for fan-backed music when pricing is transparent. Cost-effective FreeClear tier provides entry point, while proven engagement improvements drive upgrades to real music licensing.

Music Recommendation & Discovery Risk

Challenge: Inability to effectively guide clients toward alternatives when HardToClear tracks are unavailable

Mitigation: Basic tagging and essential data integration enables recommendation system showing similar tracks across tiers. Platform demonstrates how tiered system provides multiple access points to similar music, while data-assisted curation helps brands discover effective alternatives that maintain campaign authenticity and performance standards.

Operational Scaling Risk

Challenge: Manual licensing bottlenecks and potential surge in EasyClear requests

Mitigation: Email-based EasyClear approval system for major rightsholders streamlines requests. Acrylic Label's music community provides trusted individuals for demand surges.

Catalog Development Risk

Challenge: Insufficient rightsholder adoption for EasyClear/PreClear tiers

Mitigation: Pre-MVP launch strategy includes building EasyClear catalog with popular sports tracks. Acrylic Label controls 2K+ FreeClear tracks with active community outreach.
